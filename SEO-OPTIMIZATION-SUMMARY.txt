## Content Optimization

### SEO Implementation Strategy
✅ **Correct Approach:**
- Keywords in meta titles (hidden from users)
- Keywords in meta descriptions (search results only)
- Keywords in image alt tags (accessibility + SEO)
- Keywords in content paragraphs (natural placement)
- Keywords in blog articles (organic integration)

❌ **Avoided:**
- Keyword stuffing in visible headings
- Unnatural title modifications
- Forced keyword placement in H1 tags
- Disrupting user experience for SEO

### Keyword Density
- Primary keywords appear 3-5 times per page in content
- Natural placement in:
  - Meta tags (primary location)
  - Content paragraphs
  - Image alt tags
  - Blog articles
  - Supporting descriptions

### Heading Structure
✅ Clean, user-friendly headings maintained
✅ Original titles preserved for better UX
✅ SEO keywords in meta tags only
